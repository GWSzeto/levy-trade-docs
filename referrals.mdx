## TL;DR

| Tier   | Relationship       | Your cut |
| ------ | ------------------ | -------- |
| **L1** | Direct invite      | **10%**  |
| **L2** | Invitee of your L1 | **3%**   |
| **L3** | Invitee of your L2 | **1%**   |

## How it works

1. **Share your link.** Anyone who joins with it is an **L1** — you bank **10%** of every fee they pay.
2. **They share theirs.** When an L1 brings in a new user, that newcomer is your **L2**. You collect **3%** on all their fees.
3. **The snowball rolls on.** If an L2 refers someone, that person is your **L3**. You'll earn **1%** on their fees.

That's it — three simple levels. Earnings are tracked automatically as your referrals trade.

### Rakeback

Your personal rakeback rate is based on your level. The higher your level, the more of your own trading fees we send right back to you.

| XP Level | Rakeback |
| -------- | -------- |
| 0 – 19   | **5%**   |
| 20 – 39  | **10%**  |
| 40 – 59  | **15%**  |
| 60 – 79  | **20%**  |
| 80 – 94  | **25%**  |
| 95 +     | **30%**  |

## Checking & Claiming Rewards

- Use **`/invite`** to view your referral code, invite link, and your live L1, L2, and L3 stats.
- Use **`/rewards`** to view both your referral earnings and your rakeback in one place.
- You must have at least **10 USDC** available before you can submit a claim.
- The bot will show you if a claim is already pending or processing.
