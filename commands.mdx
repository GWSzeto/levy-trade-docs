### Getting started

- `/start` — creates your managed wallet if needed, shows your deposit addresses, and gives you a short onboarding guide.
- `/wallet` or `/deposit` — shows your ETH and Solana deposit addresses along with your live perps and spot balances. You can also refresh balances from the inline button.

### Trading

- `/long` and `/short` — open a perp position. Use `/long <ticker> <leverage> <margin>` or run the command with no arguments to use the interactive flow.
- `/close` — close a position. Use `/close <ticker> <percentage>`, `/close all <percentage>`, or run `/close` to pick a position first.
- `/pair` — open one long and one short in the same flow. Format: `/pair <long-ticker> <leverage> <margin> <short-ticker> <leverage> <margin>`.
- `/limit` — place a limit order. Format: `/limit <long/short> <ticker> <leverage> <price> <margin>`, or run `/limit` to use the interactive flow.
- `/stoploss` and `/takeprofit` — place trigger exits on an open position.
- `/orders` — shows your open orders and lets you cancel them.
- `/positions` — shows your open positions. You can also use `/positions @username` to view another user's positions.

### Markets and pricing

- `/markets` — shows the available perp markets, sorted by 24-hour notional volume, with pagination.
- `/chart` or `/c` — shows a chart for a token. Use `/chart <ticker> [timeframe]`. Supported timeframes are `1m`, `5m`, `1h`, `12h`, `1d`, and `1w`.
- `/price` — shows the latest price for a token. Example: `/price BTC`.
- `/hype` — quick shortcut for the HYPE price.
- `/alert` — sets a price alert. Example: `/alert HYPE $20`.

### Leaderboards and performance

- `/leaderboard` or `/leaderboards` — shows the leaderboard for the current group chat. This command is group-only. Supported timeframes are `1d`, `7d`, `30d`, and `all`. `week` is accepted as an alias for `7d`.
- `/playerleaderboards` — shows the global player leaderboard across the bot. It uses the same timeframes as the group leaderboard.
- `/pnl` — shows your realized PnL chart. You can also use `/pnl @username`, `/pnl 30d`, or combine them.
- `/stats` — shows your volume, trade count, win rate, PnL, level, rakeback, and direct referral stats.
- `/level` — shows your current level, XP progress, fee tier, rakeback, cards unlocked, and next reward milestone.

### Rewards and referrals

- `/invite` — shows your referral code, invite link, L1/L2/L3 counts, and total referral earnings.
- `/rewards` — shows your claimable referral earnings and rakeback. Claims require at least 10 USDC.
- `/points` — shows your points balance, rank, next distribution, and total points distributed. See [Points](/points) for more details.

### Wallet and account management

- `/send` — sends USDC to another user from a managed wallet. You can use `/send @username <amount>`, reply to someone's message with `/send <amount>`, or use the interactive flow. Minimum send amount is 5 USDC. Aliases: `/tip`, `/heal`, `/revive`.
- `/withdraw` — withdraws USDC from a managed wallet to either Hyperliquid or Arbitrum. You can use `/withdraw <address> <amount>` or the interactive flow.
- `/transfer` — moves USDC between perps and spot. Use `/transfer spot <amount>` for perps to spot, `/transfer perps <amount>` for spot to perps, or the interactive flow.
- `/accounts` — shows your linked accounts, lets you add API accounts, switch your primary account, and remove API accounts. Adding an API account is done in DMs.
- `/settings` — opens the settings menu. From there you can view stats, accounts, balances, withdrawals, transfers, key export, security, and support.
- `/security` — DM-only command for enabling or disabling TOTP 2FA on a managed wallet.
- `/support` — opens the support chat.
