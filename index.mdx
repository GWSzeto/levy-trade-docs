Levy Trade is a Telegram bot that enables leveraged perpetual futures trading and spot trading on [Hyperliquid](https://hyperliquid.xyz) directly within Telegram conversations.

Trade with friends, compete on leaderboards, and level up through trading volume — all without leaving Telegram.
