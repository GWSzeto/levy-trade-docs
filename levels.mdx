Levy Trade features a 99-level progression system. Earn XP through trading volume to unlock cards, fee discounts, and rakeback rewards.

## How XP Works

- **1 XP per $1 notional volume traded** (both opens and closes count)
- Higher leverage trades generate more XP per dollar of margin, but carry higher liquidation risk
- Each level requires 10.5% more XP than the previous one

### XP Formula

```
xp_per_level(L) = 7,400 × 1.105^L
cumulative_xp(L) = 7,400 × (1.105^L - 1) / 0.105
```

## Rakeback Rewards

Rakeback returns a percentage of your trading fees back to you, based on your level. Rakeback unlocks at key milestones:

| Level | Rakeback |
|-------|----------|
| 0 — 19 | 5% |
| 20 | 10% |
| 40 | 15% |
| 60 | 20% |
| 80 | 25% |
| 95 | 30% |
