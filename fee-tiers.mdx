Levy Trade fees are calculated using three components that stack together: **level-based discounts**, **30-day volume discounts**, and **per-trade leverage discounts**.

## Base Fees

| Type | Fee |
|------|-----|
| Perpetual futures | 0.05% per trade (open and close) |
| Spot buys | Free (0%) |
| Spot sells | 2x the effective perp fee (~0.10% base) |
| Bridging / deposits | No fee |
| Liquidations | No fee |

| 39 | 0.022% — 0.037% |
| 50 | 0.021% — 0.036% |
| 58 | 0.020% — 0.035% |

| 72 | 0.018% — 0.033% |
| 77 | 0.017% — 0.032% |

## 30-Day Volume Discounts

Your rolling 30-day trading volume applies an additional discount on top of your level-based fee tier.

| 30-Day Volume | Discount |
|---------------|----------|
| Under $10M | 0 bps |
| $10M+ | -0.3 bps |
| $25M+ | -0.6 bps |
| $100M+ | -0.9 bps |
| $500M+ | -1.2 bps |
| $1B+ | -1.5 bps |

## Per-Trade Leverage Discount

Each trade receives a dynamic discount based on effective leverage (notional / equity). This discount applies on both opening and closing trades.

| Effective Leverage | Discount |
|-------------------|----------|
| 0x | 0 bps |
| 10x | 1 bps |
| 20x | 2 bps |
| 30x | 3 bps |
| 40x+ | 4 bps (max) |

The discount scales linearly between these thresholds.

## Fee Floor

Total fees can never go below 0 bps. Any combination of discounts that would result in a negative fee is capped at zero.

## Spot Fee Calculation

Spot sell fees are calculated as **2x your effective perpetual fee rate**. Spot buys are always free.
