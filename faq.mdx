<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How are my private keys stored?">
    Private keys are generated locally on our server, then immediately encrypted using **Google Cloud KMS** and stored as ciphertext. Keys are only decrypted on-demand for transaction signing, then immediately discarded from memory. You retain exclusive access to your funds and can export your private key at any time via `/settings`.

    You can also enable biometric 2FA via the `/security` command for additional protection.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I withdraw my funds?">
    You have two options:

    1. **Direct withdrawal:** Use the `/withdraw` command to send funds to another Hyperliquid L1 wallet address.
    2. **Key export:** Export your private key through `/settings`, import it into MetaMask or any EVM-compatible wallet, and use [Hyperliquid's portfolio page](https://app.hyperliquid.xyz/portfolio) to bridge USDC to Arbitrum.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I connect an existing Hyperliquid account?">
    Yes. Use the `/accounts` command, then press **"Add New Hyperliquid Account"**. You'll need to enter your wallet address and generate an API key from Hyperliquid.

    Note that API-connected wallets have limited functionality — they are not eligible for the points program.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are the trading fees?">
    - **Perpetual futures:** 0.05% per trade (on both opens and closes)
    - **Spot buys:** Free
    - **Spot sells:** ~0.10% (2x the effective perp fee)
    - **Bridging and deposits:** No fee
    - **Liquidations:** No fee

    Fees decrease as you level up and increase your trading volume. See [Fee Tiers](/fee-tiers) for the full breakdown.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the minimum deposit?">
    Bridging requires a minimum of **10 USDC**. You can deposit via:
    - **Hyperliquid L1** (native)
    - **Arbitrum** (bridged automatically)
    - **Solana** (bridged via deBridge)

    After sending funds, run any command in the bot to trigger deposit processing if it hasn't arrived yet.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I sent the wrong asset (SOL, ETH, USDT, etc.) — can I recover it?">
    Yes. Go to `/settings` and export your EVM or Solana private key. Import the key into a compatible wallet (MetaMask for EVM, Phantom for Solana) to access and recover the assets.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Any trading tips?">
    Don't get liquidated.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
