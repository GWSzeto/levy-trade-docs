---
title: "Points"
---

The Levy Trade points program uses a fixed supply of **1,000,000 points**.

Points are distributed every week on **Saturday at 00:00 UTC**. Each weekly trading distribution uses a **10,000 point pool** until the full supply has been handed out.

Weekly trading points are split by trading volume for that period. On the first distribution, the bot counts all historical trading volume. After that, it uses the last 7 days of recorded volume.

Only users whose primary account is a **Levy Trade-managed wallet** are eligible for points. API-connected accounts do not earn points.

Direct referrers also earn a bonus on top of the trading pool: you get **1 point for every 5 points** your L1 referrals earn.

Use `/points` to see your balance, rank, next distribution date, and total points distributed so far.
