The Levy Trade points program rewards active traders with a share of a fixed point supply, distributed weekly.

## Overview

- **Total supply:** 1,000,000 points
- **Distribution:** Weekly on Saturdays
- Weekly distributions scale based on overall platform activity

## Earning Points

- Active trading on Levy Trade generates points
- **Referral bonus:** Earn 1 point for every 5 points your referred users earn
- Distribution amounts adjust based on total user activity across the platform each week

## Eligibility

<Warning>
  Only wallets created natively through Levy Trade (managed wallets) are eligible for points. API-connected wallets are **excluded**.
</Warning>

To check your points balance, use the `/points` command.

## Important Notes

- Levy Trade reserves the right to modify point distributions at its sole discretion
- Points are tied to your Levy Trade managed wallet
