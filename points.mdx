---
title: "Points"
---

The Levy Trade points program began on July 27, 2024. Points are distributed every week on Saturdays, based on your activity and referral contributions.

You earn 1 point for every 5 points your referred users earn. Only wallets created through Levy Trade are eligible for points — wallets connected via API key are excluded.

There are a total of 1,000,000 points. Weekly point distributions adjust based on overall user activity. As of February 15, 2025, all points have been distributed.

Bonus points are awarded to the top 10 clans every week. Clans must have at least 3 active members at the time of the snapshot to qualify for bonus distributions.

Levy Trade reserves the right to modify previous point distributions under its sole discretion.
